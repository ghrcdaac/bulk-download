About Us

With the loss of anonymous FTP, options for downloading data has become more difficult more most users. In 2018, the Data Use Team lead an effort to explore better methods for downloading data.

To solve this problem, EarthData Bulk Downloader was created for the NASA EarthData Search Client website by Information Technology Systems Center at The University of Alabama in Huntsville.
